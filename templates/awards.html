---Awards---

Create and manage employee recognition awards.


Create Award

Create three types of awards for employees including Employee of the Month, Employee of the Quarter, and Employee of the Year. After an award is created, the online tool will automatically send a PDF certificate to the recipient via email.

Manage Awards

The online application provides you with a full history of all awards previously created. You can manage these awards by deleting awards from the system.

Edit Profile

Edit your profile to update the data you entered during registration.
